ABOUT

I'm Sagar, and I'm an artist interested in virtual production and the future of interactive entertainment.

Recently, my friends and I built a VR animation tool and made a short film with it. These two projects mean a lot to me - I love making movies and building tools that make it easier to produce creative work.

These days, I write a weekly newsletter about animation and real-time tools. If you're a reader, I'd love to hear your feedback.

Thanks for dropping by. Feel free to get in touch via email or Twitter.
