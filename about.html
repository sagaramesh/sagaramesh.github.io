About

I'm Sagar, and I'm an artist based in California. I love making movies and building tools that make it easy to produce creative work.

As a student, I spent my final year building a VR animation tool and using it to make my thesis film. Those projects represent the kind of work I love - hacking with small teams, pushing the limits of today's tech, and making art in new ways.


These days, I work at Oculus on the team responsible for Quill, Venues, and Oculus TV.

On the side, I’m building projects that showcase the power of real-time animation. I’m using tools I build to animate loops and produce shows with my friends.

Thanks for visiting. Feel free to get in touch via email or Twitter.
