About

I'm Sagar, and I'm an artist based in California. I love making movies and building tools that make it easy to produce creative work.

Last year, my friends and I built a VR animation tool and made a short film with it. These days, I work at Oculus, animate loops, and write a newsletter about animation and real-time tools. If you're a reader, I'd love to hear from you.

Thanks for visiting. Feel free to get in touch via email or Twitter.
